The BTEC ICT unit 1 The Online World course covers all topics from the specification.

These topics are:

Learning Aim A

- Investigate online services and online communication.
- Online Services
- Online Documents
- Online Communication

Learning aim B:

- Investigate components of the internet and how digital devices exchange and store information.
- The Internet
- Worldwide Web
- Email
- Data Exchange
- Data Storage

Learning aim C:

- Investigate issues with operating online.
- Possible Threats to Data
